    Flamecharm
    Thundercall

There are another unique element called the Shadowcast element that you can get from obtaining the following item and follow up direction.

Shadowcast

Obtain five Umbral Obsidian

You need to bring those item to the depth and search for a hidden NPC

Additional Information:

You choose "No Attunement" at the beginning and awaken your element later is possible by collecting ingredients and heading to current location of island where the element NPC are at.

Collect those ingredients to create potion for you to awaken your hidden element ability.
