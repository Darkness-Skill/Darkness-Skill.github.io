Description

>> Deepwoken is a difficult game with permanent character loss. Losing characters is a part of the game that should be expected. <<

Seek out your destiny upon an unforgiving sea and unravel the mysteries of a dying world. Discover and modify powerful abilities, find unique strengths, and develop your character into a force to be reckoned with. Desperately try to escape the horrors that lurk just beneath the waves. Whatever you do, it's your story to be told.

No Attunement

Can only get no attunment at the start of the game when you choice your starter stats

Shadowcast

There are another unique element called the Shadowcast element that you can get from obtaining the following item and follow up direction.

Obtain five Umbral Obsidian

You need to bring those item to the depth and search for a hidden NPC.

Additional Information:

You can choose "No Attunement" at the beginning and awaken your element later in the mid or late game progression is possible by collecting ingredients and heading to a certain location of island where the element NPC are at.

Collect those ingredients to create potion for your hidden element ability awakening.
